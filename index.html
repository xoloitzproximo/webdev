Rene Almazan

CEO and Founder of Tech Hero

Rene has been in the technology sector for four years. His introduction to technology was through the YEAR-UP program in Chicago where he enrolled as a student in 2017. This is a comprehensive training program in technology and professional development that requires dedication and perseverance from its students. The first six months of the program is divided into phases, including interpersonal relationship skill development and computer curriculum in Help-Desk and information security. This is followed by a six-month internship where students gain real life experience and exposure to other technology career professionals. As Alumni, YEAR-UP graduates can access additional career advice and educational training opportunities through a network of more than 20,000 advisors and professional contacts. After completing the YEAR-UP program, Rene worked as a Tech Support Specialist for a staffing agency in downtown Chicago and has done contract work for Peapod E-Commerce Service Delivery. Rene also completed a certificate program at Northwestern University in Cybersecurity and has a CompTIA Security+ Certification. Rene is skilled in Windows, Linux and MAC-OS environments.
